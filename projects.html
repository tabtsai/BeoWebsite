Design an Icon

beginner level

Iconography is the science of identifcation, description, classification, and interpretation of symbols, themes, and subject matter in the visual arts. Icons serve as an important visual aid in any graphical communication. Their primary function is to serve as a common visual language, removing the possibility of open interpretation or ambiguity.

In this project, you will be designing a set of icons of your choice. If you're not sure what topic to create icons for, here are some possible topics.

  • Education
  • Space
  • Medical
  • Ocean
  • Travel
  • Food

    Here is my submission for the Design an Icon project. I wanted to do a space theme because I've always been interested in that area and I thought that by creating icons for it I could show my love for the extraterrestrail space world.

    I wanted my icons to be very simple and basic, while also having a fun and almost doodle feeling to them. I decided to go with the line art aesthetic for simplicity, and then added the blurry, purplish background for the that spacey vibe. Lemme know what you guys think and if you have any suggestions!
